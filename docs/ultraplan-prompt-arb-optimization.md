# Ultraplan ÔÇö Arbitrage subsystem perf optimization (the ~100 ms/tick arb phase)

**Goal:** cut the arbitrage tick phase's fixed ~100 ms/tick in the commit-bound, single-threaded bot loop, via a **sequenced set of flag-gated, default-off, byte-identical-off, CK-soak-gated levers** ÔÇö so every step is freeze-safe (nothing changes until validated + flipped, exactly like the just-shipped buy-stop / short-open batches). Conservation (CK=0) is sacrosanct. Do **not** touch the FX house economics.

## Ôśů Diagnosis (code-grounded from a sweep ÔÇö build on this, don't re-derive)
The `_phArbUs` phase is **EXECUTION-dominated (DB commits), not the in-memory scan.** ~5 arb bots (seeded UserIds 20003ÔÇô20007, `ExcelSeedService.cs:345-383`), each acting per `DecisionInterval` (default 1 s, `AIUser.cs:53`) does, in `ArbitrageDecisionService.RunAsync` (`:71`, bots iterated ascending AiUserId `:84`):
1. **Flatten** (`TryFlattenAsync :296`) ÔÇö loops **all** cross-listed candidates, reads position + both books, market-sells residual inventory.
2. **2-leg round-trip** (`TryRoundTripAsync :226-249`) ÔÇö leg-1 `PlaceTrueMarketBuyOrderAsync :233` Ôćĺ **its own root commit/fsync**; leg-2 `PlaceTrueMarketSellOrderAsync :246` Ôćĺ **a second root commit**. Opportunity scan = `CollectOppsAsync :174` (reads both USD+EUR top-of-book per candidate per bot every tick via `ReadTopAsync`/`GetAsync :148`, gap in `EvaluateDirection :160` off `_fxRates.GetBidAsk`, keep Ôëą `MinArbitrageRatePrc :193`, `PickWeighted` ÔłŁ rate┬▓ `:369`).
3. **FX rebalance** (`MaybeRebalanceAsync :323` Ôćĺ `_portfolio.ConvertAsync :361`) on the bot's `ConversionCadenceSeconds` clock ÔÇö pays `ConvertSpread` into the house (20002).

**Cost model:** each arb leg Ôëł **5 ms/fsync** (its own tx) vs ~0.6 ms plain. 5 bots ├Ś 2 legs Ôëł **50 ms**, + the **unbatched per-bot `ConvertAsync` rebalance commits** (now firing every cadence since `ConversionSkewBand` was lowered to 0.03), + occasional flatten sells. The scan is real but secondary ÔÇö and mostly wasted, because the **FX mid only re-ticks once per 60 s** (`FxRateService.cs:12`, `Tick :103`).

## What to design ÔÇö sequenced by risk/value (each its OWN commit + CK soak)

**Phase 0 ÔÇö MEASURE (no code).** Flip `BatchLegs=true` + `Db:GroupCommit:Enabled=true` together; `Bots:PhaseTimingSeconds>0`; 45-min CK soak on a short/arb-active fleet. Split scan-vs-exec empirically ÔÇö the one uncertainty is the per-bot cross-listed watchlist size (seed-dependent). **This sizes Phases 1-3.** GATE: CK=0; record the arb-phase ╬ö. *If the flag-flip alone reclaims most of the ~50 ms leg cost, Phases 1-3 may be deferable ÔÇö let the number decide.*

**Phase 1 ÔÇö batch the FX-rebalance `ConvertAsync`** (`Bots:Arbitrage:BatchRebalance`, default-off). Collect the cohort's per-tick rebalance conversions and settle them in one amortized tx (mirror the leg/short-open batch). ÔÜá **house-credit atomicity:** batching changes *when* the house (20002) is credited the spread ÔÇö the per-conversion fund credit must stay conservation-exact (no ledger drift; watch `CK_Funds` + house-revenue accrual, not just aggregate CK).

**Phase 2 ÔÇö event-driven arb scan** (`Bots:Arbitrage:EventDriven`, default-off) ÔÇö the structural centerpiece. Replace the per-tick `CollectOppsAsync` fan-out with a **lock-free watcher mirroring `HostedServices/StopTriggerWatcher.cs`** (subscribe `IMarketDataService.QuoteUpdated :156`, `OnQuoteUpdated :209`): maintain a cross-listed `(stockId)Ôćĺlive FX-adjusted gap` map, react **only** when a cross-listed quote (or the 60 s FX re-tick) moves the gap past `MinArbitrageRatePrc`; the arb tick early-outs when nothing crossed. De-churns ~99 % of scans + naturally throttles how often the legs fire. **Pure read-path ÔÇö no settlement change Ôćĺ low conservation risk.** GATE: round-trips still fire on real opps (behavior-equivalent), CK=0, arb-phase ms down.

**Phase 3 ÔÇö minor caching** (fold in or its own commit): cache the **static** cross-listed set (`CrossListedWatchlist :135` re-runs `IsListedIn` ├Ś2/candidate/bot/tick); gate `TryFlattenAsync :296` on a has-any-inventory flag (skip the all-candidates loop when the cohort is flat ÔÇö round-trips net flat); hoist the per-60 s-constant FX bid/mid lookups to once per tick.

**OUT OF SCOPE (note for Kiesh ÔÇö NOT this ultraplan):** scaling the arb cohort 5 Ôćĺ 20-30 (the Expansionist realism upside: tighter cross-currency spreads + a structural forcing-function against LLN herding). That's a **reseed + behavior change**, not perf ÔÇö bundle into the final reseed.
